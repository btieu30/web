About UMI

Tierra Wilson, better known as UMI, was born in Seattle to an African-American father and Japanese mother. Her stage name, UMI, is her Japanese middle name, which translates to "ocean". The 21 year-old is well-known for her ability to blend unique music genres such as R&B, neo-soul and lo-fi music to make hits. UMI officially began her career in 2017, with the song "Happy Again", which has garnered over 3 million plays on the streaming platform known as Spotify and 400,000 views on Youtube.
