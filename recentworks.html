UMI's Recent Works

Recently, UMI revealed she is currently working on new music, after the release of her EP or extended play, "Introspection" on June 21, 2020. However over the last two months, UMI has released acoustic versions of her most popular songs, such as "Remember Me" and "Butterfly". Click the link below to listen to the acoustic version of "Butterfly" on Youtube. In terms of her activity that isn't related to music, UMI has been releasing episodes of a self-made series titled "Full Moon with UMI". In this series, she discusses different theories associated with the spirtual world everytime there is a full moon. She has also been gradually releasing self-recorded audio videos for songs off her EP, "Introspection". Some of songs included are "Broken Bottle" and "Bet".
